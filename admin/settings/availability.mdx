Control when specialists are available for procedures and consultations.

## Procedure Availability

Manage when each specialist can perform procedures at each location.

![Procedure availability](/images/admin_procedure_availability.png)

### Viewing availability

1. Select a **Specialist**
2. Select a **Location**
3. View the calendar showing allocated hours

The calendar shows:

- **Green shading** — Available capacity
- **Patient icons** — Booked procedures
- **Monthly summary** — Total allocated hours and procedures booked

### Setting availability

Click on any day to manage availability for that date.

![Procedure availability form](/images/admin_procedure_availability_form.png)

The popup shows:

- **Allocated** — Total hours available
- **Booked** — Hours already scheduled
- **Remaining** — Available hours
- **Booked Procedures** — List of scheduled procedures

Click **Update Availability** to change the allocated hours for that day.

## Consultation Availability

Manage when each specialist is available for consultations.

![Consultation availability](/images/admin_consultation_availability.png)

### Viewing availability

1. Select a **Specialist**
2. View the calendar showing available slots

The calendar shows:

- **Green shading** — Available time slots
- **Consultation icons** — Booked consultations
- **Monthly summary** — Total allocated time and consultations booked

### Setting availability

Click on any day to manage consultation slots.

![Consultation availability form](/images/admin_consultation_availability_form.png)

Each slot shows:

- Time range (e.g., 10:45 AM - 11:00 AM)
- Available methods (Zoom, WhatsApp, FaceTime, In-person)
- Booked consultations

Click **+ Add Slot** to add a new availability slot for that day.

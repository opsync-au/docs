Manage the hospitals and day surgeries where procedures are performed.

![Locations list](/images/admin_locations.png)

## Location list

Each location card shows:

- Location name
- Address
- Phone number
- Parking availability

## Adding a location

1. Click **+ Add Location**
2. Enter location details:
   - Name
   - Address
   - Phone number
   - Website (optional)
   - Parking information
3. Click **Save**

## Viewing location details

Click **View Details** on any location to see the full information.

![Location detail](/images/admin_location.png)

The detail page shows:

- Full address with map
- Contact information
- Parking details
- Edit and delete options

## Editing a location

1. Click **View Details** on the location
2. Click **Edit Location**
3. Update the details (name, address, phone, website, parking)
4. Click **Save**

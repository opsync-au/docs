The dashboard gives you an at-a-glance view of your practice activity.

![Dashboard](/images/admin_dashboard.png)

## Alerts

Important items requiring attention appear at the top. Click **Expand** to see details.

## Specialist summaries

Each specialist has their own section showing:

### Booking stats (Last 14 days)

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Started** | Bookings where patient has begun the process |
| **Awaiting Confirmation** | Bookings waiting for consultation and procedure to be confirmed |
| **Awaiting Consent** | Bookings waiting for patient consent |

Filter by booking type: **All**, **In-Practice**, **Rapid Access**, **Recall**

Click **View all** to see the full bookings list filtered to that specialist.

### Upcoming Procedures

Calendar view of scheduled procedures for the next two weeks. Numbers indicate how many procedures are booked on each day.

Click **View all** to see the full procedures list.

### Upcoming Consultations

Calendar view of scheduled consultations for the next two weeks.

Click **View all** to see the full consultations list.

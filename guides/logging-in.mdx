Log in to your admin portal at your practice's unique URL:

```
https://admin.opsync.com.au/your-practice-name
```

Your practice URL was provided when your account was set up. If you've forgotten it, contact support.

![Login screen](/images/admin_login.png)

## Signing in

1. Go to your practice URL
2. Enter your email address
3. Enter your password
4. Click **Sign In**

## Forgot your password?

1. Click **Forgot password?** on the login screen
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a reset link
4. Follow the link to set a new password
